Today's learning— Resume Optimization with AI.

Your resume is not just a document.
 It’s your first impression — often judged by machines before humans.
Today, I learned how to use AI to turn a basic resume into an ATS-friendly, recruiter-ready profile.

 Key Learnings
 1. ATS matters more than I thought
 If your resume isn’t ATS-friendly, it may never reach a recruiter.
 2. Clarity > Complexity
 Simple, structured bullet points are easier to scan than long paragraphs.
 3. Keywords are crucial
 Using the right technical and role-based keywords significantly improves visibility.
 4. Impact-based writing wins
 Instead of writing “Worked on a project” →
 Write “Built X using Y, improving Z by %”
 5. One-page resume is powerful
 Concise, relevant, and focused resumes perform better.
