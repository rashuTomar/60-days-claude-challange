# Day 16 - Stock Fundamental Research Skill

## Overview

Built a custom Claude Skill that performs structured fundamental analysis of listed companies using reusable workflows. The skill generates consistent, evidence-based stock research reports by analyzing financial metrics, business quality, valuation, growth, and risks.

## Features

* Fundamental analysis of stocks
* Valuation assessment (P/E, P/B, EV/EBITDA)
* Financial health evaluation (ROE, ROCE, Debt, Cash Flow)
* Growth trend analysis
* Peer comparison
* Ownership and shareholding analysis
* Risk and opportunity identification
* Automated infographic-style report generation

## Workflow

1. Input stock name/ticker.
2. Collect financial and business data.
3. Analyze valuation, growth, profitability, and leverage.
4. Compare with industry peers.
5. Summarize strengths and watch-points.
6. Generate a visual research report.

## Example

Analyzed **Tata Consultancy Services (TCS)** and generated a comprehensive stock research infographic covering:

* Company Snapshot
* Valuation Metrics
* Financial Strength
* Growth Trends
* Ownership Structure
* Peer Comparison
* Overall Fundamental Quality

## Key Learnings

* Designing reusable AI workflows
* Building domain-specific custom skills
* Structuring financial research using AI
* Automating multi-step analysis processes
* Generating consistent outputs through prompt engineering

## Tech Stack

* Claude Custom Skills
* Prompt Engineering
* Financial Research Frameworks
* AI Workflow Automation

## Outcome

Created a reusable stock research assistant capable of analyzing and presenting fundamental insights for any listed company in a standardized format.
