# 🚚 Operation Lifeline: Supply Chain Crisis Lab

An interactive, browser-based supply chain management simulator where players respond to real-world logistics disruptions, negotiate with suppliers, make executive decisions, and invest in AI technologies to build a resilient supply chain.

Built as a **single self-contained HTML application** using **React (CDN)**, **Babel JSX**, **HTML**, **CSS**, and **Vanilla JavaScript**. No installation, backend, APIs, or build tools required.

---

## 📖 Overview

Supply chains are complex systems where a single disruption can affect production, inventory, deliveries, and customer satisfaction.

**Operation Lifeline** transforms these challenges into an engaging simulation that helps users understand how strategic decisions influence business outcomes during a crisis.

Each playthrough generates a different company, crisis, and outcome, making every session unique.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🏢 Dynamic Company Generation

Every simulation begins with a randomly generated fictional company including:

* Industry
* Annual Revenue
* Manufacturing Facilities
* Warehouses
* Supplier Network
* Inventory Days
* Lead Time
* Countries of Operation

---

### 🚨 Random Crisis Engine

Face unexpected disruptions such as:

* Factory Fire
* Supplier Bankruptcy
* Port Strike
* Cyberattack
* Flood
* Raw Material Shortage
* Political Conflict
* Shipping Delay

Each crisis includes:

* Urgency Level
* Business Impact
* Operational Context

---

### ⚔️ War Room Decision Simulator

Respond to the crisis by selecting **three strategic actions** from six available options.

Business metrics update dynamically through animated progress bars:

* Cost
* Inventory
* Profit
* Delivery Speed
* Customer Satisfaction

---

### 🤝 Supplier Negotiation

Participate in a four-round negotiation where every decision impacts:

* Trust
* Price
* Lead Time

A final negotiation score reflects the effectiveness of your strategy.

---

### 👔 CEO Boardroom

Answer five executive-level multiple-choice questions covering:

* Crisis Leadership
* Business Strategy
* Risk Assessment
* Customer Management
* Financial Decision Making

Receive a leadership score based on your decisions.

---

### 🤖 AI Strategy Planning

Choose two AI investments from:

* Demand Forecasting
* Inventory Optimization
* Supplier Risk Monitoring
* Warehouse Vision
* Procurement Copilot

View the projected business impact of your technology investments.

---

### 📊 Executive Dashboard

At the end of the simulation, receive a complete performance report including:

* Overall Crisis Score (0–100)
* Leadership Score
* Negotiation Score
* Resilience Rating
* Cost Control
* Risk Management
* Customer Satisfaction
* Biggest Mistake
* Best Decision
* Expert Recommendation
* Lessons Learned

## 🚀 Getting Started

1. Download the project.
2. Open the HTML file in any modern web browser.
3. Click **Start Simulation**.
4. Navigate through each stage of the crisis.
5. Review your executive performance dashboard.
6. Replay to experience a completely new scenario.

No installation or internet connection required after opening the file.
