Day 14 - AI Job Red Flag Detector
Overview

Built an AI-powered Job Red Flag Detector that analyzes job descriptions and hiring processes to help job seekers identify potential risks before applying.

Key Features
Detects unrealistic skill and experience requirements
Identifies burnout and hustle-culture indicators
Flags vague responsibilities and hiring risks
Evaluates remote job authenticity claims
Assesses overall job and company risk levels
Generates smart interview questions for validation
What I Learned
Prompt engineering for structured job analysis
Risk assessment using AI
Extracting meaningful insights from job descriptions
Converting qualitative information into actionable feedback
Using AI to support career decision-making
Output Structure
Overall Risk Score (0-100)
Top Red Flags
Positive Signals
Risk Breakdown by Category
Final Verdict (Apply / Apply with Caution / Avoid)
Smart Interview Questions
Key Takeaway

AI can help job seekers go beyond matching skills with requirements by identifying hidden risks, improving decision-making, and saving time during the application process.
