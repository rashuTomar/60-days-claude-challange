🧠 Cognitive Pattern Explorer

Cognitive Pattern Explorer is an interactive, psychology-inspired self-reflection web application designed to help users explore their thinking tendencies through engaging scenarios, drag-and-drop activities, and personalized insights.

Unlike traditional quizzes, this experience feels like an interactive journey—encouraging curiosity, reflection, and personal growth in a calm and game-like environment.

Educational Purpose: This application is designed solely for self-reflection and learning. It does not diagnose, assess, or provide medical or psychological advice.

🌟 Overview

Understanding how we think can help us make more intentional decisions.

This application guides users through a multi-chapter experience where they explore different thinking tendencies by interacting with realistic scenarios, prioritizing values, and mapping their thought processes.

Every interaction contributes to a personalized reflection report that highlights thinking preferences using supportive and non-judgmental language.

🧩 Thinking Tendencies

The application explores five educational thinking styles:

🔍 Analytical Thinker
❤️ Emotional Intuitive
🔄 Overthinking Loop Style
⚡ Action-First Decision Maker
⚖️ Balanced Reflective Thinker

Results are presented as tendencies rather than fixed labels, promoting self-awareness instead of judgment.
