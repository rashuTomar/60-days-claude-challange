# Think Like a Marketing Strategist: Grow This Brand

An interactive, beginner-friendly marketing strategy simulator built as a **single-file HTML application**.

Instead of generating marketing content instantly, this simulator teaches users **how marketers think** by guiding them through real strategic decisions.

Designed for students, entrepreneurs, creators, marketers, and anyone interested in understanding marketing strategy.

---

# Features

## Three Ways to Play

* 🏢 Use My Own Business
* 🙋 Build My Personal Brand
* 🎲 A New Client Has Arrived (Random Business Generator)

Each playthrough creates a unique learning experience.

---

# Learn Marketing Step by Step

The simulator teaches users how to:

* Understand a business or brand
* Identify the target audience
* Analyze marketing challenges
* Choose the right social media platforms
* Build content pillars
* Create a 30-day growth strategy
* Handle unexpected marketing events
* Review strategic decisions

Every major section explains:

* **What is this?**
* **Why does it matter?**

making it suitable even for complete beginners.

---

# Personal Brand Mode

Users without a business can build their own personal brand.

The simulator helps users think about:

* Their expertise
* Their story
* Their niche
* Their audience
* Their positioning
* Their long-term content strategy

Platform recommendations prioritize:

* LinkedIn
* X (Twitter)
* YouTube
* Newsletters

Content pillars include ideas such as:

* Thought Leadership
* Personal Story
* Audience Education
* Behind the Scenes

---

# Random Client Generator

Every replay generates a different client with randomized:

* Industry
* Target audience
* Budget
* Competitors
* Marketing challenge

This makes every session unique and replayable.

---

# Platform Strategy

Users choose which social platforms should be used.

The simulator explains why each platform is:

* A good choice
* A poor choice
* Better suited for different audiences

Instead of simply giving answers, it explains the reasoning behind every recommendation.

---

# Content Pillars

Users receive multiple content pillar options but must select only three.

Each pillar includes an explanation of:

* What it is
* Why it matters
* Which marketing goals it supports

This teaches strategic prioritization rather than content generation.

---

# 30-Day Growth Roadmap

The simulator creates a high-level four-week marketing roadmap.

Instead of generating daily posts, it focuses on:

* Weekly objectives
* Strategic priorities
* Growth milestones

For personal brands, Week 1 focuses on:

* Defining a Point of View (POV)
* Optimizing profile and bio
* Building a clear niche

---

# Marketing Events

Every playthrough includes a random marketing event.

Examples include:

* Viral content
* Podcast invitation
* Public disagreement
* Someone copying your content
* Sudden follower growth

Users must decide how to respond and learn the consequences of each choice.

---

# Growth Report

At the end of every session, users receive a complete strategy report including:

* Audience Understanding
* Platform Strategy
* Content Strategy
* Growth Potential
* Best Decision
* Biggest Mistake
* Three Marketing Lessons

Personal Brand mode emphasizes:

* Authenticity
* Consistency
* Niche clarity

---

# Prompt Engineering Integration

After every major section, users receive a reusable **"How to Ask Claude"** prompt.

These prompts teach users how to use AI more effectively while learning marketing strategy.

For Personal Brand mode, prompts are customized using the user's name and niche instead of a company.

# Getting Started

1. Download or clone this repository.
2. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser.
3. Choose a business mode.
4. Make strategic marketing decisions.
5. Learn from the final Growth Report.
6. Replay to experience different business scenarios.

No installation required.
