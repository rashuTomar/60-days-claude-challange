Today, I learned how AI can help optimize a resume based on a specific Job Description (JD) and significantly improve its ATS (Applicant Tracking System) score.
Key Learnings:

✅ ATS optimization is not about stuffing keywords—it's about aligning your existing skills and experience with the requirements of the role.

✅ A resume should be tailored for every application by highlighting the most relevant projects, skills, and achievements.

✅ AI can identify missing keywords, skill gaps, and improvement opportunities that recruiters and ATS systems look for.

✅ Small changes in wording, section organization, and keyword placement can make a huge difference in resume visibility.
