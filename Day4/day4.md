Today, I learned one of the most powerful Prompt Engineering techniques: Chain-of-Thought (CoT) Prompting.
Instead of asking AI to give a direct answer, we encourage it to think through the problem step by step before reaching a conclusion.

This approach helps AI:
 Break down complex problems
 Improve reasoning accuracy
 Reduce mistakes
 Generate more logical and detailed responses
