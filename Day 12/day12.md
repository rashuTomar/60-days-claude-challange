# Day 12 – Job Search & Personal Branding with AI

## What I Learned
Learned how AI can help create a complete job search toolkit beyond resume optimization.

## Key Learnings
- ATS-friendly resume positioning
- Cover letter and outreach email generation
- Referral and follow-up messaging
- Personal branding and recruiter positioning
- Interview talking points and skill gap analysis

## Key Takeaway
A strong resume gets attention, but personal branding, networking, and communication create opportunities. AI can streamline the entire job search process and improve professional visibility.

## Skills Explored
ATS Optimization, Personal Branding, Career Positioning, Networking, Professional Communication
