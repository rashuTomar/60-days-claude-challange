Using AI effectively is not just about writing better prompts. It's also about choosing the right model and reasoning level for the task.
Today I explored Choosing the right Claude model and reasoning effort level.
Before today, I used AI tools without thinking much about which model should handle a task. But I learned that selecting the right model can significantly improve both speed and output quality.

💡 Key Learnings:

✅ Haiku is best for quick answers, simple explanations, and fast iterations.

✅ Sonnet is the ideal daily driver for coding, debugging, learning, and web development.

✅ Opus shines when solving complex problems, making architecture decisions, and performing deep research.
