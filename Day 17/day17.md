Project: AI Vehicle Fuel & Cost Analysis Dashboard

Built an AI-powered dashboard that:

Analyzes vehicle fuel and maintenance data from CSV files
Compares Petrol, Diesel, CNG, EV, and E85 fuel types
Visualizes fuel costs, CO₂ emissions, and maintenance expenses
Calculates E85 cost-benefit metrics and break-even analysis
Generates responsive and interactive charts
Provides vehicle-specific recommendations and insights
