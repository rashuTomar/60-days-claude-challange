**Day 27/60 of my #60DaysClaudeChallenge 🚀**

Today, I used **Claude** to build an **interactive Prior Authorization Story Simulator**—a web application that simplifies one of the most complex workflows in the US healthcare system through an engaging, story-driven experience.

Rather than reading long documents, users learn the entire Prior Authorization (PA) process by progressing through an interactive conversation—from a doctor's visit to insurance review, denial, appeal, and final approval.

### 🏥 What I built

* Interactive story with 8 guided scenes
* Chat-based educational interface
* Progress tracker for the complete PA journey
* Decision-based interactions after every scene
* Beginner-friendly explanations of healthcare operations
* Clean healthcare-inspired UI built using HTML, Tailwind CSS, and Vanilla JavaScript

### 💡 Key Learnings

* Claude can generate highly structured, interactive educational applications when provided with detailed prompts.
* Prompt engineering plays a huge role in translating complex business workflows into intuitive user experiences.
* Even non-traditional domains like healthcare can be explained effectively through storytelling and interactive simulations.
* Building with AI isn't just about generating code—it's about providing clear context, constraints, and user experience requirements.
* Well-crafted prompts can significantly reduce development time while maintaining code quality and consistency.

Every day in this challenge is helping me become better at collaborating with AI to transform ideas into fully functional applications.
