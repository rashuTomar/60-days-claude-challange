# AI Decision Strategist

An AI-powered decision-making assistant designed to help users think more clearly about difficult choices. Instead of providing instant advice, the application guides users through a structured interview, uncovers hidden assumptions, evaluates every option objectively, and generates a professional interactive **Decision Report**.

---

# 🚀 Features

## 🧠 Guided Decision Interview

The assistant asks only **four carefully designed questions**, one at a time, to understand:

* The decision you're facing
* Your available options
* Your goals and timeline
* Your intuition and concerns
* Your biggest fear and the reversibility of the decision

This structured approach minimizes unnecessary conversation while maximizing clarity.

---

# 📊 Comprehensive Decision Report

After collecting the responses, the AI generates a complete interactive dashboard.

---

## 📌 Section 1 – The Real Decision

Summarizes:

* The actual decision
* The core trade-off
* Why the decision feels emotionally difficult

---

## ⚖️ Section 2 – Case for Every Option

Each option receives its own analysis card including:

* Strongest argument in its favor
* Hidden advantages
* Biggest weakness
* Best suited for specific priorities and values

---

## 🧩 Section 3 – Assumption Buster

Helps challenge flawed thinking by identifying:

* Common assumptions
* Cognitive biases
* Important factors being overlooked

Biases may include:

* Loss Aversion
* Confirmation Bias
* Status Quo Bias
* Sunk Cost Fallacy
* Availability Bias

---

## 📈 Section 4 – Decision Matrix

Every option is scored across seven key dimensions:

* Life & Career Upside
* Financial Safety
* Growth & Learning
* Stress Level
* Reversibility
* Long-Term Alignment
* Regret Risk

Includes:

* Animated progress bars
* Total score out of 70
* Automatic winner highlighting

---

## 🚨 Section 5 – Premortem Analysis

For the top two options, the report imagines failure after twelve months and identifies:

* Possible reasons for failure
* Early warning signs
* Preventive actions

This encourages proactive thinking before committing.

---

## 📅 Section 6 – 7-Day Test Plan

Instead of rushing into a decision, users receive a structured one-week validation plan:

* Research tasks
* Small real-world experiments
* Conversations to have
* Decision criteria for Day 7

---

## 🏆 Section 7 – Final Verdict

Provides:

* Recommended option
* Supporting rationale
* Conditions that could change the recommendation
* One hard truth to consider

The verdict is based solely on the information provided by the user.

---

## 📸 Section 8 – Shareable Cards

Creates three screenshot-friendly summary cards:

* Decision Matrix Summary
* Final Recommendation
* LinkedIn-ready reflection post

Perfect for sharing insights with others.
