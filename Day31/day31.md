# AI Supply Chain Control Tower

An interactive browser-based operations simulation that puts you in the role of the **Head of Operations** for a global supply chain organization.

Monitor live business KPIs, respond to operational disruptions, prioritize critical alerts, and make strategic decisions under time pressure. Every action influences business performance, requiring players to balance cost, service, customer satisfaction, and operational efficiency.

---

## Overview

Supply chains face unexpected disruptions every day—from supplier delays and weather events to inventory shortages and transportation failures.

This simulation recreates those real-world scenarios in an engaging management game where every decision has consequences.

Your objective is to maximize operational performance before the timer runs out while maintaining healthy business KPIs.

---

## Features

### Live Operations Dashboard

* Real-time KPI monitoring
* Premium dark Operations Control Center UI
* Animated KPI cards
* Responsive dashboard layout

### Business KPIs

Track key operational metrics throughout the game:

* Service Level
* Customer Satisfaction
* Inventory Health
* Transportation Efficiency
* Operating Cost
* Revenue Protected
* Overall Score
* Remaining Time

### Dynamic Operational Alerts

Random operational events including:

* 🚨 Port Congestion
* 🚨 Supplier Delay
* 🚨 Truck Breakdown
* 🚨 Warehouse Running Out of Stock
* 🚨 Customs Inspection
* 🚨 Demand Spike
* 🚨 Factory Machine Failure
* 🚨 Weather Disruption
* 🚨 Wrong Inventory Count
* 🚨 Damaged Shipment

Each alert includes:

* Priority level
* Business impact
* Time remaining
* Operational description

### Decision-Based Gameplay

Choose the most appropriate response for every disruption.

Available actions include:

* Expedite Shipment
* Use Backup Supplier
* Reroute Trucks
* Increase Production
* Transfer Inventory
* Approve Air Freight
* Delay Decision
* Ignore

Every action produces different outcomes affecting:

* Business KPIs
* Operational score
* Future operational risks
* Delayed consequences

### Increasing Difficulty

As the game progresses:

* Alert frequency increases
* Multiple disruptions occur simultaneously
* Decision-making becomes more challenging
* Operational pressure continuously rises

### End Game Analytics

After the timer ends, receive a complete performance report including:

* Final Score
* Performance Grade (A+, A, B, C, D)
* Final KPI values
* Total Alerts Resolved
* Correct Decisions
* Incorrect Decisions
* Operational performance summary

### Additional Features

* Pause Game
* Help & Instructions Modal
* Sound Toggle (visual)
* Live Event Log
* Priority-based alert colors
* Animated UI
* Responsive design for desktop and mobile

## Technology Stack

* HTML5
* CSS3
* Vanilla JavaScript

No external libraries, frameworks, APIs, or backend services are used.

The entire application runs from a **single HTML file** and works completely offline.

## How to Run

1. Download or clone this repository.
2. Open the HTML file in any modern web browser.
3. Start managing operations and respond to real-time supply chain disruptions.

No installation, dependencies, or internet connection is required.
