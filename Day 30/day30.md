# 📦 Supply Chain Builder

An interactive, browser-based learning simulator that teaches the fundamentals of supply chain design through hands-on decision making.

Instead of reading lengthy explanations, players build a supply chain step by step while seeing how each decision impacts business performance in real time.

Built as a **single self-contained HTML application** using **React (CDN)**, **Babel JSX**, **HTML**, **CSS**, and **Vanilla JavaScript**. No installation, backend, APIs, or build tools required.

---

# 📖 Overview

Every business relies on a supply chain to move products from suppliers to customers.

A well-designed supply chain can reduce costs, improve delivery speed, minimize risks, and increase customer satisfaction.

**Supply Chain Builder** introduces these concepts through an interactive simulation designed for complete beginners. Before every decision, the application explains what the concept means, why it matters, and the trade-offs involved.

---

# ✨ Features

## 🏢 Random Company Generator

Each playthrough begins with a unique fictional company featuring:

* Industry
* Products
* Countries Served
* Demand Level

Every simulation provides a different business scenario.

---

## 🎓 Beginner-Friendly Learning

Before every decision, players receive simple explanations covering:

* What the concept means
* Why businesses care about it
* Advantages and disadvantages
* How the decision affects overall business performance

No prior supply chain knowledge is required.

---

## 🏗️ Build Your Supply Chain

Design your company's supply chain by selecting:

* Supplier Strategy (Single or Multiple Suppliers)
* Factory Location
* Warehouse Strategy
* Transportation Method

  * Road
  * Rail
  * Sea
  * Air
* Inventory Strategy

  * Low Inventory
  * Balanced Inventory
  * High Inventory

Each choice directly influences business outcomes.

---

## 📊 Live Business Metrics

Business performance updates instantly after every decision through animated progress bars.

Tracked metrics include:

* 💰 Cost
* 🚚 Delivery Speed
* ⚠️ Risk
* 😊 Customer Satisfaction
* 🌱 Sustainability

Players immediately see the impact of every strategic decision.

---

## 📈 Final Executive Dashboard

At the end of the simulation, players receive a complete business assessment including:

* Overall Supply Chain Score (0–100)
* Strengths
* Weaknesses
* Biggest Operational Risk
* Three Practical Improvement Recommendations

The dashboard summarizes the effectiveness of the designed supply chain.

# 🚀 Getting Started

1. Download the project.
2. Open the HTML file in any modern web browser.
3. Click **Start Building**.
4. Review your generated company.
5. Make supply chain decisions step by step.
6. Watch the business metrics update live.
7. Complete the simulation to receive your executive dashboard.
8. Replay to experience a new company and scenario.

No installation or internet connection is required.
