# 🕵️ Marketing Detective

An interactive detective-style learning game that teaches **marketing analysis and strategic thinking** through fictional business cases.

Instead of reading marketing theory, players investigate campaign failures, examine evidence, connect clues, and identify the real marketing mistake before revealing the solution.

Built as a **single-file HTML application** for a smooth, standalone experience.

---

## ✨ Features

* 🎨 Theme selection with multiple color presets
* 🕵️ Detective-inspired interface
* 📁 Randomized fictional marketing cases
* 📌 Interactive investigation board
* 🧩 Draggable evidence system
* 📊 Campaign metrics analysis
* 💬 Customer feedback investigation
* 📱 Social media performance review
* 🎯 Identify the primary marketing mistake
* 📖 Detailed learning report after every case
* 📈 Progress indicators and smooth animations
* 📱 Responsive design
* 🌐 Runs completely offline in a browser

---

## 🎮 Gameplay

1. Receive a new marketing case.
2. Review the company profile and campaign objective.
3. Analyze campaign metrics and marketing channels.
4. Examine customer comments and social media performance.
5. Organize evidence on the investigation board.
6. Identify the primary marketing mistake.
7. Reveal the case solution.
8. Read the learning report and suggested improvements.
9. Replay to investigate another randomly selected case.
